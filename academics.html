Courses / Activities

Clara Mohammed School of STL engages in such coursees as the following.

  • Reading Comprehension / Vocabulary
  • Language Arts / Writing
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Environmental Science
  • Social Studies
  • Islamic Studies & Quranic Studies
  • Foreign Languages
  • Character Development
  • Tutorial Math & Reading
  • PE

Technology is embedded into all of our courses to make every student ready for the real world. Students will enroll in coursework at appropriate grade level and given opportunity for specific coursework designed to meet their individual needs. Field trips, sports program, and after school extra-cirricular activities are available.

Clara Mohammed School also engages in community partnership activities, which is part of every grade level program, to enhance the goals of the school mission. The activities are conducted throughout the regularly scheduled school day and after school.
