Tuition

The tuition at clara Mohammed School of St. Louis is set at $4000 annually for the 2018-19 school year and approved by the Board of Directors on 13th, day of July, 2017. An option is provided by CMS to vide the annual tuition into monthly payments of 10 equal payments in the amount of $400 per month. If the parent wishes to select the first option of paying annual tuition of $4000 at one time in August of 2018, there will be a 5% discount available in the amount of $200 reduction of tuition
