Financial Aid

Clara Mohammed School of St. Louis is now accepting students who are eligible for Clara Mohammed School Income-Based Scholarship for the 2018-19 school year. Parents can work with Clara Mohammed School office manager to complete and submit the application.

The deadline to apply for the Clara Mohammed Scholarship Program is June 1, 2018. Because the application process can be lengthy, parents of eligible children should contact the school as soon as possible.

There is one scholarship per grade level available, which pays 50% of tuition for the 2018-19 school year for eligible students. Clara Mohammed School scholarships $2000 per school year (50% are worth up to of tuition) for grades kg-5. Once a student receives a scholarship, he or she can continue to renew the scholarship each school year as long as they meet eligible criteria.

For more information or to find out if your student is eligible, contact the office manager of Clara Mohammed School at 314-339-6267.
